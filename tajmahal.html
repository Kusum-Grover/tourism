Taj Mahal, India

Overview

The Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World, is a timeless symbol of love and architectural brilliance. Located in Agra, India, this white marble mausoleum was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal. The intricate details, symmetrical beauty, and romantic history draw millions of visitors every year. A visit to the Taj Mahal is a journey into India’s rich history and Mughal architecture.

Tour Details

Includes: Train or any flight to Agra, hotel stay, Taj Mahal sunrise tour, heritage walk, and local food tastes..

Things to Do

  • Enjoy the breathtaking sunrise view of the Taj Mahal
  • Explore the Agra Fort and Mehtab Bagh
  • Take a guided tour to learn about Mughal history
  • Shop for local marble handicrafts and souvenirs
  • Try authentic Mughlai cuisine at famous local restaurants
