Tokyo, Japan

Overview

Tokyo, the bustling capital of Japan, blends traditional culture with cutting-edge technology. From serene temples and historic districts to towering skyscrapers and futuristic shopping streets, Tokyo offers something for everyone. Whether you're exploring ancient shrines, enjoying world-class sushi, or wandering through the neon-lit streets of Shibuya, Tokyo is a vibrant city that promises unforgettable adventures.

Tour Details

Package Price: $1,599

Includes: Round-trip flights, 4-star hotel stay, guided city tour, and a Mt. Fuji day trip.

Things to Do

  • Visit the historic Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa
  • Shop in Shibuya and Harajuku districts
  • Enjoy sushi at Tsukiji Outer Market
  • Relax in the tranquil gardens of the Imperial Palace
  • Take a day trip to see Mt. Fuji
