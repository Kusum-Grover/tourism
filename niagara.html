Niagara Falls, Canada

Overview

Niagara Falls is one of the most breathtaking natural wonders in North America. Located on the border between Ontario, Canada and New York, USA, it features three powerful waterfalls: Horseshoe Falls, American Falls, and Bridal Veil Falls. On the Canadian side, you’ll enjoy stunning views, vibrant nightlife, casinos, attractions, and family-friendly activities. Whether you're cruising beneath the falls on the Hornblower, exploring Clifton Hill, or dining with a waterfall backdrop, Niagara delivers excitement and awe at every turn.
